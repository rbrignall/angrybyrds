About Angry Byrds

The Puritan Pigs have infiltrated our houses, our choirs and have filled our music with parallel fifths. Stop them before it's too late!

How to play

Use your finger or mouse to pull back the sling. Release to fire Byrd. You score points depending on what you manage to destroy, and remember: points mean parts (in this case, SATB).

This game was developed to mark the 400th anniversary of the death of Tudor composer William Byrd (c. 1540-1623). Visit Byrd Central to find out more about the man, and about other events marking the anniversary.

Angry Byrds is based on the popular game Angry Birds, and is just for fun.
